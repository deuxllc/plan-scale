FAQ

Can I measure without a reference length?

Real-world measurements need scale. Without a known length, you can only measure pixels or make a rough estimate.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. The tool works in the browser, so your image stays on your device.

How accurate is it?

Accuracy depends on image quality, calibration, and perspective. Floor plans and drawings are usually more reliable than angled photos.
