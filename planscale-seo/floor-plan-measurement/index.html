FAQ

Can I use an image without labeled dimensions?

Yes, if you know at least one real-world distance from the plan, such as a wall you measured manually.

Can PlanScale detect lines automatically?

PlanScale includes a line detection workflow for floor plans and drawings. You can remove or adjust extra lines after detection.
